About Our Post

Luneta Post 52 is dedicated to supporting local veterans, their families, and our community. We provide a place of camaraderie, assistance, and advocacy for those who have served our nation.

Veteran Support

Assisting veterans with claims, resources, and transitioning back to civilian life.

Community Outreach

Organizing local youth programs, patriotic events, and community service projects.

Camaraderie

A welcoming space for veterans to connect, share stories, and support one another.
